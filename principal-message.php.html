Principal Message
Mr. Abdul Qayoom Wani |

Dear Parents, Students, and Visitors,

Welcome to the digital gateway of Dream World Educational Institute Allachizab Kupwara! I am delighted to extend my warm greetings to each one of you through our school website.

In the fast-paced world of today, communication is key, and this website serves as a bridge between our school and the global community. It is a platform where you can explore the essence of DWEI Allachizab, our values, curriculum, and the exciting activities that shape the academic journey of our students.

Our dedicated team of educators and staff work tirelessly to create an environment where students not only receive quality education but also thrive personally and socially. As we embrace the digital era, this website becomes a window into the vibrant life of our school.

I encourage you to navigate through the various sections, stay updated on announcements, and witness the achievements of our talented students. This online space is a testament to our commitment to transparency, accessibility, and fostering a strong school community.

Best regards,

Mr. Abdul Qayoom Wani
Principal
DWEI Allachizab Kupwara.
