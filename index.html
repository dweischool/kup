Welcome To

Dream World Educational Institute Allachizab Kupwara

Established in 2009, Dream World Educational Institute has been a beacon of knowledge, nurturing young minds and fostering a passion for learning in the picturesque region of Allachizab, Kupwara. Our institution stands as a testament to the commitment to excellence in education, driven by the belief that every student has the potential to achieve great heights.
